description

Candy is a vibrant match 3 game where the general motive is to get as many points as possible by connecting candies and making combos. Once 3 candies match they are automatically cleared and points are gained. Players can also match 4 candies or connect 5 cnadies in a L shape to get special candies that gives the player extra points when activated. A level is cleared when player gains the minimal amount of points to complete the level or when a specific goal is accomplished. For example clearing 20 green candies from the screen. Once one level is cleared there is a 3 star scoring system The game board varies in a variety of sizes and shapes to keep the player engaged and challenged.
